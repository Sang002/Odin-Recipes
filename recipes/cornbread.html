Golden Sweet Cornbread

Want an amazing appetizer or dessert? You have to make this wonderful cornbread!

Ingredients Needed:

  • 1 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Cup Yellow Cornmeal
  • 2/3 Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 3 1/2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1/3 Cup Vegetable Oil

Instructions to Make this Dish:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 Degrees C).
  2. Spray or lightly grease a 9 inch round cake pan.
  3. Mix flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt, and baking powder. Stir in egg, milk and vetetable oil untill well combined
  4. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  5. Bake in oven for 20 to 25 minutes. Insert a toothpick into the center into it comes out clean.
  6. Enjoy.
